Our Story

Where Passion
Meets Precision

Founded in 2019 by former professional players, ÉLITE was born from a simple belief — that the gear you wear should be as serious about the game as you are.

Explore Collection
🏆

Crafted for the beautiful game.

ÉLITE started in a small workshop in Milan, where our founders spent months deconstructing boots worn by the world's greatest players — analysing every stitch, every synthetic panel, every carbon fibre plate — before designing their own.

Today we work with professional academies, elite training centres, and individual players across 60 countries. Our relationships with Nike, Adidas, Puma and New Balance mean you get access to the exact same gear worn at the highest levels of the sport.

We don't believe in compromise. Neither should you.
